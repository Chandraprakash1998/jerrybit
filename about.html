About Us

JerryBit is an educational project co-founded by Sandip Ghosh and Chandra Prakash Bairagi intend to teach, train and educate young generation, especially pursuing higher secondary courses in high school, computer programming and conventional robotics. Recent advancement in science and technology and tough competition from foreign countries has demanded Indians to develop its own technology.
For long time, professional computer programming and internet-based product has been only limited to engineering student. Moreover, lack of knowledge of this field devoid them from focusing on particular interest. Many high school students are interested in building things like robotics, designing and computer but lack of knowledge lead them to pursue mainstream career. Large group of mass still thinks this has no further career than building toys.
JerryBit gives highlighted knowledge and opportunities in computer programming and robotics. We, at JerryBit, gives opportunity to all pre-college student a chance to explore the modern world of communication and age of internet. We let them to explore modern technological product and give an insight how they are developed.
